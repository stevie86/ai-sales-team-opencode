# ADSENGINEER: Product Brief (AI Sales Ready)

**Category:** AI-Powered Tracking & Conversion Compliance
**Core Value Prop:** Recover 30% of lost ad data and automate GDPR-proof tracking via a sovereign AI-ready toolset.

---

## 1. Product Overview
AdsEngineer is an "AI-Ready" infrastructure for performance marketing. It replaces brittle, client-side tracking (GTM-only) with a hardened serverless stack that bypasses ad-blockers and ensures 100% data visibility.

**The "Magic Moment":** Our forensic audit tool can definitively prove a prospect is currently violating GDPR (pixels firing before consent) or losing 20-40% of their data to browser privacy features.
