### The Data Crisis

|-------|--------|
| Safari ITP | 7-day cookie cap, loses cross-domain tracking |
| Ad-blockers | 25-35% of tracking blocked |

**Result:** 35-45% of conversions are invisible to your ad platforms.

**Result:** Most websites fire tracking before consent — illegal.
