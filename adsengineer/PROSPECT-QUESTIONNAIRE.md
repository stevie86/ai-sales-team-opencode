## Pain Points & Challenges

6. **Current Challenges**
   - What are your biggest frustrations with your current tracking/setup?
   - Where do you see data discrepancies or gaps in your reporting?
   - How much time do you spend troubleshooting tracking issues?
   - Have you experienced data loss due to ad blockers, ITP/ETP, or privacy regulations?
   - What compliance concerns keep you up at night?

7. **Specific Issues**
   - Do you struggle with cross-domain tracking?
   - Have you noticed discrepancies between platform-reported conversions and your internal data?
   - How confident are you in the accuracy of your ROAS calculations?
   - Do you face challenges tracking users across devices?
   - How difficult is it to implement new tracking requirements from ad platforms?

8. **Business Impact**
   - What would improved tracking accuracy mean for your business (better budget allocation, increased ROAS, etc.)?
   - How much revenue do you estimate you might be misattributing or missing due to tracking issues?
   - What opportunities are you missing because of incomplete data?
   - How does poor tracking affect your ability to optimize campaigns?

---

## Compliance & Privacy

9. **Regulatory Landscape**
   - Which privacy regulations apply to your business (GDPR, CCPA, NDSG, etc.)?
   - What is your current compliance status with these regulations?
   - Have you received any compliance-related warnings or fines?
   - What consent mechanisms do you currently use?

10. **Data Handling**
    - How do you handle user data consent and preferences?
    - Where is your user data stored and processed?
    - Do you have data processing agreements in place with all vendors?
    - How do you handle data subject requests (access, deletion, etc.)?

---

## Decision Making & Budget

11. **Buying Process**
    - Who are the key stakeholders involved in evaluating new marketing technology?
    - What is your typical procurement process for new tools?
    - What is your budget cycle and planning timeline?
    - What is the typical timeline for evaluating and implementing new solutions?

12. **Budget & Investment**
    - Do you have a dedicated budget for marketing technology/tools?
    - What is your typical investment range for marketing analytics solutions?
    - How do you measure ROI on marketing technology investments?
    - Are you currently evaluating any other solutions in this space?

---

## Goals & Objectives

13. **Success Metrics**
    - What would success look like if you implemented a better tracking solution?
    - What specific metrics would you want to improve?
    - What business outcomes are you trying to drive?

14. **Ideal Solution**
    - What features would your ideal tracking/advertising solution have?
    - How important are real-time capabilities vs. batch processing?
    - What level of customization do you need?
    - How important is ease of implementation and ongoing maintenance?

---

## Next Steps

15. **Evaluation Process**
    - Would you be open to a technical assessment of your current setup?
    - What information would you need to see to move forward with a proof of concept?
    - What would constitute a successful pilot or trial period?
    - What are your concerns about implementing a new solution?

---

*Use the insights from this questionnaire to:
- Tailor your AdsEngineer pitch to the prospect's specific situation
- Quantify the potential impact of solving their pain points
- Identify the right stakeholders to engage
- Build a compelling business case
- Address objections before they arise*
