## Why AdsEngineer Is a Perfect Fit for Babyrella

### 1. GDPR Risk - Children's Data is the Highest Stakes
Austrian e-commerce selling **children's products** is under the strictest GDPR scrutiny. Under GDPR Article 8 and Austrian DSG, processing data related to children requires extra care. If their Meta Pixel fires before consent acceptance, they are not just facing a standard GDPR violation - they are collecting data in a context involving children's products, which regulators treat with zero tolerance. Fines scale to severity.

### 2. They Run Paid Ads (Confirmed)
Kerstin explicitly manages "Babyrella-Werbeanzeigen" (paid advertising). They are spending money on Meta/Google Ads. Every conversion their pixel misses due to ad-blockers or iOS privacy = wasted ad spend. With products ranging from 10 EUR (magnets) to 350 EUR (furniture/creative cubes), missed high-value conversions hurt their ROAS significantly.

### 3. Shopware Platform = Complex Tracking
Shopware is less plug-and-play than Shopify for tracking. Server-side tracking implementation is more manual, meaning they likely rely on basic client-side GTM setup that is vulnerable to ad-blockers and privacy browsers.

### 4. Seasonal Revenue Concentration
Baby/children's e-commerce has extreme seasonal peaks:
- **Easter** (March/April) - current peak
- **Back to School/Kindergarten** (August/September)
- **Christmas** (November/December - likely 30-40% of annual revenue)
- **Baby showers** (year-round but clustered)

Losing 30% of conversion data during Christmas = losing 30% of their best advertising optimization window.

### 5. Subscription/Rental Model = Recurring Revenue Tracking
Their "Miete" (rental) service via Mollie subscriptions needs accurate attribution to know which ad brought in a subscriber. One missed subscription attribution could mean months of unattributed recurring revenue.

### 6. Multi-Channel Marketing = Attribution Complexity
They use: Instagram, Facebook, Newsletter, WhatsApp, Paid Ads, and likely Google Shopping. Without proper server-side tracking, cross-channel attribution is guesswork.
