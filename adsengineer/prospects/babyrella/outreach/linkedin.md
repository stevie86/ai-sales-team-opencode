**Note on Chrome Cookie Hook:** Google reversed the full Chrome cookie phase-out in 2024-2025. Instead, Chrome introduced user-choice privacy controls (third-party cookies remain on by default but users can disable). However, Safari ITP and Firefox ETP already block third-party cookies entirely, and ad-blocker adoption continues to climb. The urgency is real — frame it as "browser privacy is accelerating" rather than a single Chrome deadline.
